Hello, and welcome to one of my homes on the web! I’m Petar, a Research Scientist at DeepMind. I have a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge (Trinity College), obtained under the supervision of Prof Pietro Liò. My research interests involve devising neural network architectures that operate on nontrivially structured data (such as graphs), and their applications in algorithmic reasoning and computational biology. In particular, I am the first author of Graph Attention Networks—a popular convolutional layer for graphs—and Deep Graph Infomax—a scalable local/global unsupervised learning pipeline for graphs. My research has been featured in media outlets such as ZDNet.
